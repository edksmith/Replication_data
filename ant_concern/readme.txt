This is the replication files for the paper "Concern and anticipation of sea-level rise provide grounds for social tipping towards climate action".

Included are:
- ant_concern_microdata_USA.dta: an extract of relevant variables for this study generated from the survey "Climate Change in the US Spring 2021".
- ant_concern_microdata_USA.do: a Stata do file to replicate Figures 5 and A3, as well as Tables S4 and S5.
- ant_concern_microdata_questionnaire: the full survey instrument questionnaire
- ant_concern_microdata_surveyflow: an overview of the survey flow and respondent splits adopted for this research design
